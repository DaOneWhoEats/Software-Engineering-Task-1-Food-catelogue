Sautéing - Quick and Delicious

Sautéing

Sautéing is a method of cooking that involves frying ingredients quickly in a small amount of oil or butter. It is often used to cook vegetables and small pieces of meat.

Grilling - Perfect Char

Grilling

Grilling involves cooking food on a rack over a heat source, typically an open flame. It is popular for preparing meats and vegetables, giving them a distinct smoky flavor.

Baking - Mastering the Oven

Baking

Baking is a dry heat method of cooking, usually in an oven. It is often used for preparing bread, cakes, pastries, and casseroles.

Blanching - Locking in Freshness

Blanching

Blanching is a process where food, typically vegetables, are briefly boiled and then plunged into ice water to stop the cooking process. This helps preserve their color and texture.

Braising - Tender and Flavorful

Braising

Braising involves cooking food slowly in a closed container with a small amount of liquid. This method is ideal for tough cuts of meat, as it tenderizes them while adding flavor.
